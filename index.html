A browser-based 2D MMORPG where battles are fought offchain and treasures are claimed on Stellar's Soroban smart contracts.

Explore dungeons, fight enemies, and mint rare loot as NFTs. Trade, vote, and build the future of the world with your fellow adventurers.

Play Demo

🎮 Game Features

EpochQuest brings retro-style dungeon crawling to your browser, with real-time multiplayer battles, AI-driven NPCs, and procedurally generated dungeons.

Players earn loot based on damage dealt, with rewards distributed onchain using secure Soroban smart contracts. Rare items become NFTs. Common items are tradable offchain.

🌐 Built on Stellar

EpochQuest uses the Stellar blockchain and Soroban smart contracts to mint, store, and trade in-game items.

Players connect via the Freighter Wallet extension to claim loot, trade items, and vote on game upgrades via onchain governance mechanisms.

We use Soroban’s NFT and token standards to ensure interoperability and transparency while keeping gameplay fast and efficient offchain.
